Permanent quattro® Drive

Audi's legendary permanent all-wheel drive distributes engine force dynamically. A mechanical self-locking center differential splits torque 40:60 front-to-back under normal driving, adapting instantly to route up to 70% forward or 85% rearward when slippage occurs.

Dynamic All-Wheel Steering

Combining direct responses with peerless stability. At lower speeds, the rear wheels turn up to 5 degrees in the opposite direction of the front wheels, shrinking the turning circle by up to 1 meter. At high speeds, they turn in the same direction to boost lane-change stability.

48-Volt Mild-Hybrid (MHEV)

Supercar dynamics, smart efficiency. A 48V starter/generator can recover up to 12 kW of power during deceleration, storing it in a lithium-ion battery. This system allows engine-off coasting at highway speeds for up to 40 seconds to reduce emissions and fuel usage.

RS Adaptive Air Suspension

The standard RS adaptive air suspension is specially configured for high performance, sitting 20mm lower than standard. Above 120 km/h, the ride automatically drops another 10mm to optimize aerodynamics and cornering control.
